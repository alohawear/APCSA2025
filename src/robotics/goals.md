## 2025-2026 Season Goals

### Learning Objectives

#### Technical Skills
- **Mechanical**: Learn CAD, fabrication techniques, and assembly best practices
- **Programming**: Master FTC SDK, implement computer vision, optimize autonomous
- **Electrical**: Understand control system, wiring standards, and troubleshooting
- **Strategy**: Develop game analysis skills and competition strategy

#### Professional Skills
- **Teamwork**: Collaborate effectively across sub-teams
- **Communication**: Present to judges, sponsors, and community
- **Project Management**: Meet deadlines, track progress, manage resources
- **Problem Solving**: Debug issues under pressure, iterate on designs

### 3-Year Goals
- Build a sustainable team with strong recruitment pipeline
- Develop a recognized team brand and reputation

- Celebrate effort and learning, not just wins

- Have fun while working hard
