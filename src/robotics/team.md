### Our Mission

To inspire young people to be science and technology leaders by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.

### Core Values

1. **Gracious Professionalism** - Competing with respect and kindness
2. **Coopertition** - Displaying cooperation and competition simultaneously
3. **Innovation** - Pursuing creative and elegant solutions
4. **Teamwork** - Working together toward common goals
5. **Fun** - Enjoying the experience and celebrating achievements
