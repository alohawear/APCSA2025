## Welcome to Our FTC Robotics Team

The **Martin Luther King Jr. Academic Magnet** First Tech Challenge (FTC) robotics team combines engineering, programming, and teamwork to become better computer scientists.

### What is FIRST Tech Challenge?

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics competition for students in grades 7-12. Teams design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in an alliance format against other teams. Each season features a new game challenge that requires strategic thinking, engineering skills, and collaboration.
